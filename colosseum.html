Colosseum

Our first stop is the mighty Colosseum! This ancinent building is the largest ancient amphitheatre ever built, and is still the largest standing amphitheatre in the world! The Colosseum's contruction began under emperor Vespasian and was completed in 80 AD under emperor Titus. It could hold an estimated 50,000 to 80,000 spectators and it was used for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles including animal hunts, executions, re-enactments of famous battles, and dramas based on Roman mythology, and briefly mock sea battles.
