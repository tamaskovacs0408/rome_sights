Rome

"Such life of yore the ancient Sabines led,
Such Remus and his brother: Etruria thus,
Doubt not, to greatness grew, and Rome became
The fair world's fairest, and with circling wall."

Vergilius: Georgics II. 532-535.

Rome, the capital of Italy is one of the most beautiful city in Europe and the center of the ancient Roman Empire. It's located on the central-western part of the Italian-Peninsula, along the shores of Tiber. You can find a piece of histroy from the last 2000 years in almost every corner of the city.
